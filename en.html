FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Won't accounts get banned right away?
+
We use warmed-up Premium accounts with history and gradual ramp-up. Limits are strictly observed: message caps, delays between dialogs, randomized actions. After 10+ years we've perfected the protocol — ban rate is at statistical noise level.
Will people really not realize it's AI?
+
Our data shows 95% of people see the agent as a real manager. GPT is tuned to your voice, uses natural pauses and even typos — making it feel human. If someone asks "are you a bot?" — the agent gracefully deflects and redirects.
How long does launch take?
+
Standard launch is 3–5 business days from briefing. We build the audience database, train the agent on your product, write scenarios and test dialogs. You'll see first replies from potential clients on day one.
Will it work for my niche?
+
We've worked across B2B and B2C: renovation, real estate, marketplaces, e-learning, tourism, legal, fitness, e-commerce and dozens more. If your audience is on Telegram — we'll find them. The free demo includes a real sample of your target audience.
How do I monitor conversations?
+
You have full access to your dashboard with every conversation in real time. Join any dialog with one click and continue personally — the agent smoothly hands it over. All hot leads are auto-tagged and added to a separate list.
What's included in the free demo?
+
The demo includes: 500 real contacts from your Telegram target audience, a configured agent for your product for a test run, and 3 ready-made strategies with projected results for your niche. No obligations — just see how it works.
Telegram is restricted — how do you operate in Russia?
+
Yes, Telegram is formally restricted in Russia, but it doesn't matter: 65M+ Russians actively use it via VPN, proxies and bypass tools. Plus a huge Russian-speaking audience outside Russia uses it freely. This is a loyal, paying audience — that's exactly who we work with. Importantly: our tool is not advertising in any legal sense — we emulate natural human communication, comply with legislation and don't violate platform rules.
Do you work with WhatsApp, Viber and other messengers?
+
Yes, we can work with WhatsApp, Viber, Instagram and other platforms — but on custom terms. It's technically more complex and takes longer than Telegram. If you need a system for your specific stack — leave a request, we'll discuss your needs and build a tailored solution.
